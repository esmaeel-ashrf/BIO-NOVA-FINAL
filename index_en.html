Introduction

The Black Soldier Fly is one of the insects gaining increasing attention in the fields of agriculture and environmental sustainability. This interest stems from its remarkable ability to convert organic waste into valuable products such as animal protein and organic fertilizer, in addition to its role in enhancing environmental sustainability.

In this article, we will explore everything you need to know about breeding the Black Soldier Fly—from how to establish and care for them, to the challenges and solutions, as well as future applications and environmental benefits. Keep reading to discover how this insect could be the key to sustainability and a circular economy.
